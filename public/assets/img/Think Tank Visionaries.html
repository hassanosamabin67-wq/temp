Think Tank Collaboratives

Brainstorm, Innovate, and Build Projects Together

Think Tank Collaboratives is Kaboom Collab’s unique space for collective brainstorming and project-building. It’s a place where visionaries in every field—music, art, entertainment, and beyond—can come together to share ideas, solve creative challenges, and bring new projects to life.

Why Use Think Tank Collaboratives?

Collaborative Brainstorming

Meet with other creatives to share ideas, explore new concepts, and brainstorm in a supportive, interactive space.

Project-Building Support

Whether you’re working on a film, a collaborative art project, or an entertainment concept, Think Tank provides the structure and resources to turn ideas into reality.

Global Connections

Connect with visionaries from all over the world. Think Tank Collaboratives break down geographic barriers, allowing you to work with a diverse group of creatives with unique perspectives.

How to Get Started

Join or Create a Think Tank Session

Open a room to work on your project or join an existing collaborative session. Set your focus, invite others, and get started on brainstorming or project planning.

Share Resources and Ideas

Use virtual whiteboards, file sharing, and chat features to share concepts and get real-time feedback.

Turn Ideas into Action

Think Tank Collaboratives isn’t just for brainstorming—it’s for building. Move from ideas to action, using project management tools to keep your team on track.

Make creative breakthroughs in Think Tank Collaboratives and bring your biggest ideas to life!

Get Started

Join Kaboom Collab to elevate your artistry, connect with global creatives, and innovate through extraordinary collaborations.
